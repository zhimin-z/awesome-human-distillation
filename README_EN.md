> **What is Human Distillation?**
> Take a real person who existed in your life — a colleague, a mentor, an ex, a parent — and distill them into a callable Claude Code Agent Skill through chat logs, writing, voice, or any other trace they left behind. This is step one of the Digital Life Project.
